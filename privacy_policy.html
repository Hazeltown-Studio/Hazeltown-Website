Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2025

Hazeltown Studio ("Hazeltown," "we," "us," or "our") takes the privacy of our players very seriously. Your privacy matters to us, and we want to be upfront about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you interact with our games, websites, and services (the "Services").

By using the Services, you are agreeing to the policies outlined here. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services. We do our best to limit data collection to what's necessary for running our games and improving your experience.

We also update this policy to keep things fresh and transparent. Updates will be communicated to our users through appropriate channels. We encourage you to review this document regularly.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected when you:

  • Play our games or use in-game features.
  • Visit our websites or engage with our community.
  • Contact our support team for help.

This policy applies regardless of the platform you use to access our services, including iOS, Android, PC, or any other digital medium. If you access the Services through a third-party platform (like Google Play, the App Store, or social media), their privacy policies may also apply.

What Data Do We Collect? And Why?

Information You Provide

We aim to keep things simple and only collect the data we need to make our games run smoothly, and improve your experience. In some features, you may be asked to give a name for your avatar or team, and we will store that information. If you contact our support team, we will collect whatever details you choose to share, including your email address or a description of your issue, to help resolve your request.

Information We Collect Automatically
  • Your game data for backup or restore to run the game smoothly.
  • Device information. We automatically collect technical details, including your device model and operating system to ensure everything runs as expected, and to troubleshoot when something goes wrong.
  • Account Identifiers. To assist with improving the performance of our games, we collect information such as your device ID, IP address, and interactions with ads to improve our services.
  • Analytics. We collect what data on your interactions within our games to help us improve and iterate upon the game.
  • Purchase Data. If you any make in-game purchases, the platform through which you complete your purchase (like the App Store or Google Play) will provide us with purchase confirmations without collecting your payment details.
Information From Third Parties

Some parts of our games rely on third-party services, like ads and analytics. These services might collect info about how you play or interact with ads using cookies or similar technology.

We invite you to read their privacy policies for how your data may be used for these services:

  • ironSource - Privacy Policy
  • Firebase - Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Audience Network - Privacy Policy
  • MoPub - Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob - Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads - Privacy Policy

With your authorization, we may also collect basic third-party identifiers like your username and player ID from Game Center or Google Play Games. This lets us enable features like cloud saves, team play, and customer support, making sure you can switch devices and keep your progress effortlessly.

Information We DO NOT Collect
  • We DO NOT collect information on your location
  • We DO NOT collect or store your email (unless you contact customer service via email), phone number, address, or any other sensitive personal information
  • We DO NOT collect information about your name, age, gender, ethnicity, sexual orientation, or any health-related information
  • We DO NOT collect biometric information
  • We DO NOT collect internet, search, network activity information
  • We DO NOT collect sensor data, camera, audio, temperature, etc
  • We DO NOT collect professional or education status

How We Keep Your Data Safe

We take data security seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect any information we collect. Our data is stored in secure, managed servers, and we limit access to only those who need it to run the game. While no system is 100% foolproof, we do our best to keep your data safe from unauthorized access, leaks, or misuse.

What You Can Do With Your Data

  • Right To Access - You may contact us for details on what information we have collected for your account.
  • Right To Be Forgotten - You reserve the right to request deletion of your account data.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Personalized Ads - Our ads are managed by third-party providers. Most platforms (like iOS and Android) will allow you to opt out of personalized ads in their settings.
  • Right to Limit Data Collection - You can adjust your settings in Game Center or Google Play Games to restrict what is shared to our services.

Children's Privacy

If you are under 18, you will need your parent's permission to play our games online or use our services.

If you are under 16 and reside in Europe, or under 13 elsewhere, you can not use our online services or play our games while connected to the internet.

Our games are not designed for kids under 13, and we do not knowingly collect, use, or share information from children. If we are made aware of any unintentionally collected personal data from a child, we will delete it as soon as possible.

If you are a parent and believe your child's data was collected by mistake, please use the in-game settings to delete their account.

Data Retention

We generally keep your data indefinitely unless you ask us to delete it (see the What You Can Do With Your Data section). That said, we do not guarantee permanent records, and our backups and analytics may be cleared from time to time without notice.

If you request account deletion, we will remove your data, but please note that some data may be retained for up to 30 days before being fully erased from our systems.
